# Life

> Everyday intelligence — bridging the gap between intent and action.

A planned collection of tools and automations for daily life. Currently **on hold** while foundational capabilities in the Auxiliary and Finance categories are being developed — but the vision is clear.

## Why This Category Exists

The most impactful use of an AI agent isn't in any single domain — it's in the quiet friction of daily routines: information overload, forgotten tasks, decisions made on incomplete data, habits that slip through the cracks.

Life is where Hermes' capabilities meet everyday reality. The goal isn't to replace judgment or automate living — it's to reduce the cognitive overhead of staying organized, informed, and intentional.

## Scope

This category covers four broad areas:

### Knowledge Management
Tools for capturing, organizing, and retrieving the flood of information encountered daily — articles, notes, bookmarks, and the connections between them. The aim: surface the right context at the right time without manual filing.

### Routine Optimization
Automations that streamline recurring patterns — morning briefings, schedule reconciliation, preparation checklists, end-of-day reviews. Reduce the number of small decisions that drain attention throughout the day.

### Health & Habit Tracking
Lightweight systems for monitoring habits, reconciling data from different sources, and generating actionable insights. Not a fitness app — an intelligence layer that connects dots a human might miss.

### Planning & Logistics
Travel itineraries, event coordination, meal planning, household management — anything where gathering information, cross-referencing constraints, and producing a plan can be partially delegated.

## Current Status

**On hold.** This category is intentionally deprioritized while the system's foundational layers (Auxiliary) and first applied domain (Finance) are built and stabilized. The tools here will benefit directly from the guardrails and infrastructure being developed elsewhere.

## Principles

Every module in this category will follow the same standards that apply across HermesBoost:

- **Original craft** — nothing copied from templates or generated in bulk
- **Solves real friction** — each piece exists because a specific problem was encountered
- **Open-ended by design** — tools here are exploratory; they will evolve with needs, not toward a fixed spec

This category is a garden, not a blueprint. It will grow in the directions that prove useful.
